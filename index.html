ABOUT

Who I am & how I think

I don’t build “just another page”. Every project has a structure, a reason and a clear codebase behind it. The black & white aesthetic helps keep everything calm and focused.

Profile

I combine frontend (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, animations) and backend (PHP / Node, MySQL) so you can go from an idea to a real web application that actually works.

My structure is clear, sections are well defined and the design stays minimal. I don’t like random elements or effects that don’t support the idea.

How I work

  • Structure first

    Clean layout, separated logic and components that make sense.

  • Responsive by default

    The page must behave seriously both on mobile and desktop.

  • Subtle animations

    Effects that you feel more than you see – everything stays smooth, not distracting.
